Dressed Beans

A delicious dish of saucy beans, creamy and garlicky

These beans will make the perfect side to almost any dish.

And they're a perfect way to add some more greens to your diet!

Ingredients
  • 1.2kg mixed green and runner beans
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • extra virgin olive oil
  • red wine vinegar
Steps
  1. Peel garlic and grate into container
  2. Add the extra virgin olive oil and red wine vinegar
  3. Mix well and leave overnight
  4. Cook beans in boiling water for 7 minutes
  5. Drain beans and add dressing, then mix well
  6. Serve
