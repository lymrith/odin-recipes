Pistachio Halva

A rich, creamy dessert from Turkey

This crumbly, nutty dessert will leave you wanting more. Perfect after a spicy mediterranean dish.

Ingredients
  • olive oil
  • 50g pistachios
  • 8 cardamom pods
  • 250g tahini
  • vanilla extract
  • sea salt
  • 230g caster sugar
Steps
  1. Grease a loaf tin and line with clingfilm
  2. Roughly chop pistachios
  3. Remove cardamom seeds from pods and grind
  4. Mix together the pistachios, cardamom, tahini, vanilla and sea salt in a large mixing bowl
  5. Beat until smooth
  6. Boil the water and sugar in a saucepan, when it bubbles, reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes
  7. Add tahini to the syrup
  8. Spoon into the lined tin
  9. Leave to set overnight
